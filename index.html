Responsive Redesign

For this assignment, I identified usability, learnability, memorability, and accessibility problems with the existing website; then used those findings to develop a refined approach. I also designed a basic Bootstrap-style UI component framework from scratch, featuring text fields, several styles of button, and dropdowns.

Development

Iterative Design

For this assignment, I worked with a team to identify a recently-launched startup, and then created a mockup for a mobile app based on the description of their company. We went through multiple stages of design, including brainstorming a variety of sketches, consolidating them into a single low-fi wireframe, converting that to a high-fidelity Figma mockup, and then making design adjustments after talking to a professional designer and our peers.

BearCheck

I worked with a team to design and build a tool for creating and tracking the completion of complex, nested multimedia checklists. We developed tools to enable the checklists to change over time while still retaining the ability to audit previous sessions with the checklist.
